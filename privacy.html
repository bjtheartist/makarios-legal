Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 17, 2026

MAKARIOS ("we," "our," or "us") is a Christian discipleship mobile application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the MAKARIOS app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials. You may sign in using email/password, Google Sign-In, or Apple Sign-In.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including lessons completed, prayers read, quiz scores, streaks, and progress data. This data is used to track your discipleship journey and personalize your experience.

Journal Entries: Any journal entries you create are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Subscription Information: If you subscribe to MAKARIOS Pro, payment processing is handled by Apple (via the App Store) and RevenueCat. We do not directly collect or store your payment card information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's features and functionality
  • To track your learning progress, streaks, and discipleship journey
  • To sync your progress across devices when signed in
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To improve the app experience through anonymized analytics

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, user data storage, and analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Sanity: Content management for prayers, lessons, and other app content. Sanity Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: Payment processing for subscriptions. Apple Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

Your account and progress data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud). We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Authentication credentials are encrypted and stored using secure device storage.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your associated data within 30 days. Journal entries stored locally on your device are not affected by account deletion.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your progress data

7. Children's Privacy

MAKARIOS is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
